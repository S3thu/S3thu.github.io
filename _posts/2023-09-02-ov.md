# Overfitting and Underfitting

## Overfitting 

 - when a model has accuracy during training and give much error during testing is said to be overfitting.
    * i.e low bias and high variance

## Underfitting

 - when a model has more error rate during training and more error rate during testing is said to be underfitting.
   * i.e high bias and high variance
